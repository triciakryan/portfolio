Tricia K. Ryan Portfolio | Microlearnings

Microlearnings deliver short learning experiences with specialized content and activities. Microlearnings address one topic and objective and include one key question. The microlearning content incorporates consistent and clean visual design (simple color palette) and has a similar look, feel, and navigation features. The user flow and branching scenarios are designed by focusing on the learner’s experience. The content—presented in multiple modalities such as text, audio, video, photographs, and animations, with assessment questions—align with standards. The learners can immediately apply course material and microlearning to their work.
